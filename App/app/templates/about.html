Introducing miStudySpace

Finding a study space in the library is never going to be the same....

Search an entire library from wherever you are

miStudySpace helps you find the perfect study spot without having to physically search. Search by library, floor and region.

Location data captured only when connected to MWireless

Whether you are connected to MWireless on your phone, computer or both, your location is only accounted for once when calculating the current occupancy.

School is long, and time is fleeting

Real-time library statistics are updated every 5 minutes. In addition to real-time statistics, miStudySpace provides hourly and weekly average graphs for those who want to know what times are best to go to a library.

Mobile view only

Our app is designed for you to enjoy on your phone only, but there might be changes down the road. Make sure you are connected to MWireless on your devices to help improve our calculations.
